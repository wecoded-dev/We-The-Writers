About Us

Welcome to "We The Writer", a creative sanctuary for wordsmiths, storytellers, and thinkers. Our platform celebrates the raw, unpolished beauty of writing in its most authentic form.

We believe that the best stories often come with rough edges, sketchy outlines, and the beautiful imperfections that make them uniquely human. Our community thrives on creativity, collaboration, and the shared passion for the written word.

Join us as we explore the depths of imagination, challenge conventional narratives, and create something truly extraordinary together.

Writing Challenges

Sketchy Story Sprint

Deadline: Every Friday at 6 PM EST | Prize: Featured publication + $100

Deadline: Monthly, last day of each month | Prize: Featured publication
